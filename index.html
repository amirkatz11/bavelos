AI analyst — root cause
Endpoint count grew from 163 to 205 over 4 months but billing system quantities were never updated after initial contract setup. This is part of a systemic pattern: billing quantities are set at contract signing and never re-synced with the product system.
Recommended action
Update Zuora subscription to 205 endpoints. Generate catch-up invoice for 42 endpoints x $20 x 4 months = $3,360. Implement automated usage-to-billing sync to prevent recurrence.

Why Bavelos
Not a dashboard. An operating system.
Dashboards show you data. Bavelos runs your commercial operations overnight and tells you what needs attention.
Architecture

Add capabilities without breaking things

New billing model? New system? Register a new agent — it plugs into the existing orchestrator, uses shared memory and patterns, and emits findings through the common pipeline. Zero changes to what's already running.

Intelligence

Deterministic math, AI reasoning

The numbers are exact: contract says $5,000, billing says $4,300, delta is $700. The interpretation is AI: "This happened because of a mid-cycle plan change that wasn't propagated to Zuora." Both matter. Neither alone is enough.

Memory

Gets smarter with every customer

Per-customer memory learns billing quirks. Shared patterns encode system behaviors. Mark a false positive once — it stays suppressed. The more you use it, the less noise you see.

Integration

Works with your billing system, not instead of it

Bavelos doesn't replace Zuora or Stripe or NetSuite. It verifies they're correct. Your billing system computes the charges. Bavelos makes sure those charges are right. Every night, automatically.
